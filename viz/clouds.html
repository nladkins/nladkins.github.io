This page provides the following cloudiness data:

  1. Provides an overview of cloudiness on earth.
  2. Provides an overview of cloudiness by hemisphere.
  3. Provides some statistical data associated with the hemisphere plots.
*Conclusion*: The cloudiness is highest around the equator and in the northern latitudes. Interestingly, the cloudiness drops are similar patterns to humidity percentage decreases. The more extreme of the latitudes, the more the cloudiness seems to vary in the northern hemisphere. The cloudiness tends to not have a strong correlation with latitude in the southern hemisphere with the exception of a high-degree of cloudiness cluster at the equator (latitude = 0).
